Hedgehog

Hedgehogs have the ability to hibernate (although not all do). Hibernating means that they hole themselves up and stay in a dormant state for winter.

  • Scientific Name: Erinaceinae
  • Average Length: 20 - 30 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 2 - 5 years
  • Habitat: Savannas, forests, deserts, scrublands and suburban gardens

Hedgehogs are mammals easily recognized by their spines, which are hollow hairs made stiff with keratin. Hedgehogs are usually brown, with pale tips to the spines. They can roll into a tight ball in self-defense, causing all of the spines to point outwards.
